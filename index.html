About

Nellore's Favourite Since 2018

Creamy Inn started as a neighbourhood ice cream dream in Nellore back in 2018. What began as a single parlour with a handful of classic flavours has grown into three beloved branches across the city — each serving the same warmth, quality, and joy that made us Nellore's go-to dessert destination.

Today, Creamy Inn is much more than ice cream. Our menu spans 50+ ice cream flavours and sundae combinations, premium milkshakes, signature mocktails, fresh pizzas, burgers, Chinese food, and starters — all under one roof. Whether you're stopping by after a movie, planning a birthday party, or just craving something sweet on a hot Nellore afternoon, we've got you covered.
