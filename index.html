About the parlour

A local dairy story, now presented like a modern brand experience.

Cream More has been serving Nellore since 2018 under Sree Rajarajeshwari Dairy Foods. The refresh keeps the warmth of the original site while giving it stronger storytelling, better structure, and cleaner browsing.

The parlour sits near Ayyappa Temple in Vedayapalem and works best as a dependable neighbourhood destination: easy for families, comfortable for quick visits, and flexible enough for take-home tubs or small celebration plans.

Instead of treating the website like a poster, the new build behaves more like a small product: smoother interactions, smarter content grouping, quicker contact paths, and richer local SEO signals.
