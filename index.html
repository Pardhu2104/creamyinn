Our Story

Nellore's Beloved Ice Cream Destination

Sree Rajarajeshwari Dairy Foods has been bringing smiles to Nellore since 2018. Under our beloved Cream More brand, we craft ice creams that go beyond taste — they're experiences, memories, and moments of joy.

Located in the heart of Vedayapalem near Ayyappa Temple, our A/C parlour is the perfect spot for families, couples, and friends to unwind with a premium scoop.
